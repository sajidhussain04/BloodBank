About JharJeevan

JharJeevan Blood Bank was established in 2023 with the mission to create a robust blood donation ecosystem in Jharkhand. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring safe and adequate blood supply to all hospitals and patients in need.

Our network includes over 10,000 registered donors and we serve more than 20 hospitals across the state. We follow strict quality and safety protocols in blood collection, testing, storage and distribution.

Become a Blood Donor

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a noble act that saves lives. One donation can save up to three lives. Regular blood donation also has health benefits for donors including reduced risk of heart disease and stimulation of blood cell production.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: Minimum 12.5 g/dL
  • No major illnesses or infections
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Last donation should be at least 3 months ago

Donor Benefits

  • Free health checkup
  • Blood group testing
  • Refreshments after donation
  • Donor certificate
  • Priority during emergency

Blood Request Process

  1. Fill out the request form with all required details
  2. Our team will verify the request within 30 minutes
  3. We'll match you with available donors or blood units
  4. You'll receive confirmation with donor/collection center details
  5. Collect the blood from the designated location
